# Visual 01: Title Page Hero

Purpose:

- visual for the title slide
- communicate non-invasive optical sensing and brain monitoring

Recommended defaults:

- aspect ratio `1:1`
- photoreal / high realism
- dark editorial scientific tone
- no text, no logos, no watermark

Prompt:

```text
Create a cinematic scientific hero visual for a presentation about non-invasive intracranial pressure monitoring. Show a realistic human head in side profile or three-quarter profile in a dark editorial clinical environment. A compact optical forehead probe is placed outside the skull. The probe emits subtle near-infrared illumination, with delicate speckle-like optical texture and soft flowing signal patterns suggesting hemodynamic measurement. The subject should look calm, realistic, and medically credible. The image should feel elegant, restrained, and presentation-safe, with strong compositional clarity and enough negative space for slide layout. Use dark shadows with controlled warm amber and cool teal highlights. No surgery, no gore, no text, no logos, no clutter, no holographic overlays.
```
